Driplet - your cryptocurrency provider to the real world

Driplet — Web3 payment product that allows users to spend any Solana token in real life!

Users top up their card balance with tokens like $BONK, $WIF, $SLERF (and any SPL token), and then pay for goods or services online or offline — just like a regular bank card.

Mission
Project Mission

To make any Solana token — even the wildest memecoin — spendable in the real world. Driplet turns degen activity into everyday utility: you live in crypto, and now you can spend your crypto directly, without cashing out or swapping to fiat first.

How it works
1.
The user visits the website and connects a Phantom wallet or pastes a private key
2.
They instantly receive a free virtual Driplet Card
3.
They top up the card with SOL, USDC, or any SPL memetoken
